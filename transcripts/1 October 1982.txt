In the mosque they are talking loudly of the worldly affairs. Even while some people are saying prayers, they are not mindful of the fact that the house of Allah is for the worship of Allah. This is not the case. They are telling that Allah is Allah, Allah is Allah, Allah is Allah. This is the work of the Quran. There are three places of worship. There are three places of worship in the mosque. You should not discuss your own private affairs in the mosque. Only religious talk should be heard. And that too when the prayers have been finished. Not while the prayers, people are saying prayers, it is permitted. But you should start talking even on other religious topics. So these are the in and obligations which were not put into a model. You come here just for the memory of Allah. Remember Him in your heart or in which you are in. That is possible. But not for you to talk of various things either in worship and giving and this and that. As it is in the social place for you to eat. It is not so. It is in the house of Allah. And you should remember it and teach it to your younger generation as well. Allah bless you.
