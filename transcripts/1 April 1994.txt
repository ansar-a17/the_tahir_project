Now, I will explain these verses to you further, as I had recited them. أَفَمَنْ شَرَحَ اللَّهُ صَدْرَهُ لِلْإِسْلاَمِ فَهُوَ عَلَى نُورٍ مِنْ رَبِّهِ Is that person, whose chest Allah Himself has opened for Islam, فَهُوَ عَلَى نُورٍ مِنْ رَبِّهِ and he is a light from his Lord. This is a public announcement, a public sermon and a challenge for all human beings. If you want to be like this, then be like this and show it to you. Apart from this, every path is a path of destruction. أَفَمَنْ شَرَّهَا اللَّهُ صَدْرَهُ لِلْإِسْلاَمِ فَهُوَ عَلَى نُورٍ مِنْ رَبِّهِ This is a safe place. Apart from this, there is no safe place. Those who are going to do the mention of God have a harsh heart but there is no middle way. Those who do not do the mention of God must have a harsh heart. So there is a warning against that.
